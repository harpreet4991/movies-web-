Carry On Jatta 3

Carry On Jatta 3, also known by the initialism COJ3, is a 2023 Indian Punjabi-language
comedy film directed by Smeep Kang and produced by Humble Motion Pictures.
A sequel to both Carry on Jatta (2012) and Carry On Jatta 2 (2018), it stars Gippy Grewal
and Sonam Bajwa in the lead roles with Gurpreet Ghuggi, Kavita Kaushik, Binnu Dhillon,
Nasir Chinyoti, Jaswinder Bhalla, B.N. Sharma and Karamjit Anmol in supporting roles.
